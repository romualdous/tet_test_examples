ProcessProcess

1. Planning

Got an epiphany in the middle of a night and want to make it live?! Let us Dr. Frankenstein your idea into reality. With an assortment of experienced business professionals, we can help you structure the software into a technology miracle. Collaboration is key.

2. Design

The love for the product begins in its user-friendliness. “Does it work?” is not the only question we ask. We also ask, “Which colors makes us app-happy?”, “Is this something humans like to use?” and “Do cats prefer to swipe or scroll?” Imagination is your best friend, and it knows how to use Figma.

3. Development

Abra-cat-abra! In the words of a famous dude - “Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic.” This code you don’t have to pull out of a hat by ears nor saw it in half to understand how it works. The only thing you need to know is that we have computer wizards and they speak binary spells.

4. Testing

Indeed here we ask, “Does it run?” until the answer is “Like a hamster in a wheel.” Quality testing is not only about how long does the hamster run, but also – does it always run in the same direction? The product testing master is all about assurance of quality. Catsy cats certified.

5. Launch

Your app might not be the Falcon 9 and it may not fly to Mars, but once the product is finished and qualified, it will need to burn rocket fuel on its way to users’ hands. Our experienced paws offer business care and a software massage to support the success of even the most badass entrepreneurs.
